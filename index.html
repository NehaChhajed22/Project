About Us

The CoE has been established by the joint efforts of Bharat Forge Limited, Pune, and Vidya Pratishthan, Baramati. Establishing a CoE is a step to develop innovation and an entrepreneurial ecosystem and it will be the best place for the students where they will develop the skill sets in cutting-edge technologies and apply those to solve real-life problems. It is a step to assist innovators/ budding entrepreneurs in their start-up journey and promote an innovation ecosystem in a rural area like Baramati.

CoE consists of four labs and an incubation centre where the students will be made available with high-end workstations/computers and equipments which will help the students to convert their innovative ideas into a prototype and market viable products. In this CoE the students will be imparted with skills in cutting edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Computer Vision (CV), Robotics, and Data Analytics (DA). The facilities of Electric Vehicle and VLSI will also be provided to the students.



Objectives


  1. To nurture cutting-edge technology-based innovations, IPRs, and startups.
  2. To develop skilled human resources to serve the needs of industry and society with more focus on women empowerment.
  3. To provide need-based technology training to researchers, entrepreneurs, and the industry.
  4. To ensure the self-sustainability of CoE by identifying new revenue generation opportunities through Training, IPR, and consultancy work.


Thrust Areas of COE


  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Internet of Things
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics and 3D Printing
  • Electric Vehicle
  • Data Analytics
  • Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality

The CoE organized one day training on 3D printer technology and hands-on session by WOL 3D, Pune on 28 March 2023

A one-day workshop/training was conducted on 28th March 2023, at VPKBIET’s Bharat Forge Sponsored Centre of Excellence for youth Development on 3D printing and Design. Experts from WOL 3D Pvt. Ltd. conducted training for the students. Around 51 students along with lab in-charges attended the training session. 3D printing also called as Additive Manufacturing (AM) is a process of developing prototypes using various materials like plastics, ceramics, metals, glass and composite materials. In recent years, 3D printing has developed significantly and perform crucial roles in many applications, with the most common applications being manufacturing, medicine, architecture, custom art, and design, and can vary from fully functional to purely aesthetic applications. Knowledge of 3D printing process will enable students to convert their ideas into reality. I will also help them to develop their innovative products.

Number of Students Participated : 51
Objective of Workshop/Training :
  1. 3D model preparation using modelling software
  2. STL files generation and conversion
  3. 3D printing software and processes
  4. Hands-on Session on Fused Deposition Modelling (FDM) printer
  5. Hands-on Session on Stereolithography (SLA) Printer
  6. Hands-on Session on 3D Scanner and 3D Pens

July 2023 Activities

MOU with COEP’s BHAU Cell

MOU between Bharat Forge sponsored Centre of Excellence (CoE) and was signed with COEP’s Bhau Institute of Innovation, Entrepreneurship, and leadership, Pune on 10th July 2023.

The major highlights from the MOU are:
  1. Bhau institute will provide incubation and entrepreneurship development support to CoE.
  2. Bhau institute will provide basic mentoring support to the students and faculty members
  3. Bhau institute will help the CoE to organise seminars, webinars, workshops and competitions under the head of Bhau cell.
